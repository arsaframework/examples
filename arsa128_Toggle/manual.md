Toggle display maximum 6 SKUs.

How to use:
1. Share widget.lib to app ARSA PREVIEW.
2. Write the code on absolutely new and empty layer.
	2.1 #include <widget.lib>
	2.2 function(widget::toggle, name0, product0, name1, product1, ... , name5, product5)
	NOTE: name and product are name of layer and also must pair always.
